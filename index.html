A flagship event of FITT that serves as a premium gathering for innovation ecosystem enablers to foster groundbreaking ideas. In the 2024 edition, we spotlight the transformative power of partnerships & their crucial role in nurturing groundbreaking ventures. It also drives the importance and potential of technology commercialization for strong Industrial growth. A special significant event would be pitching for the FITT Investment Fund, a deep tech focused fund to propel the next wave of entrepreneurs towards success.
